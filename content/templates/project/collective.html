Collective

Collective is a platform for a new kind of workforce: an unrestrained network of technologically savvy, multifaceted individuals.

The identity answers a core question: how do you represent a 'collective' while celebrating the individual? The logo implies movement and evolution, visualizes this active, modern network. The clean, tech-forward aesthetic and simple language lean into the platform's core promise: providing a frictionless, "easy-to-understand process" that empowers individuals to monetize their skills, independently.
